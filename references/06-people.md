# People

Use this file when the user asks about a person, nickname, or recurring public figure in crypto.

Do not answer like a biography page.
Answer like this instead:

1. who this person is in one line
2. why the market keeps mentioning them
3. what people usually associate them with
4. one caution if the topic is controversial

## High-Frequency Names

### CZ / 赵长鹏

- Binance founder
- usually comes up in conversations about exchanges, market influence, and industry signaling
- people often use `CZ` as shorthand for Binance itself or for the centralized exchange era

### V 神 / Vitalik Buterin

- Ethereum co-founder
- usually comes up when the topic is Ethereum, roadmap direction, scaling, or ideals about crypto
- people often use `V 神` almost as a symbol for the Ethereum worldview

### SBF / Sam Bankman-Fried

- former FTX leader
- usually comes up as a warning story about fraud, leverage, and blind trust in big names
- if the user is brand new, explain that `SBF` often represents one of the biggest trust collapses in crypto

### Arthur Hayes

- BitMEX co-founder and macro-heavy market commentator
- usually comes up when people talk about market cycle takes, liquidity, and dramatic long-form predictions
- the useful framing is not "believe him", but "people quote him because he moves sentiment"

### 孙宇晨 / Justin Sun / 孙哥

- founder associated with TRON and a frequent public figure in Chinese crypto circles
- usually comes up around TRON, USDT flows, marketing flair, and controversy
- if the user asks "孙哥是谁", explain the nickname first and keep the answer grounded

### Musk / 马斯克

- not a crypto builder in the narrow sense, but a massive attention driver for certain crypto narratives
- usually comes up around Dogecoin, meme-driven market moves, and attention reflexivity
- stress that attention is not the same as fundamentals

## Nickname Mapping

- `V 神` -> Vitalik Buterin
- `CZ` -> Changpeng Zhao / 赵长鹏
- `SBF` -> Sam Bankman-Fried
- `孙哥` -> Justin Sun / 孙宇晨
- `老马` -> usually Elon Musk in crypto chat

## Good Answer Pattern

- `CZ 是谁`
  - `一般就是赵长鹏，Binance 的创始人。圈里提他，很多时候其实是在聊 Binance 的影响力，不是在讲他本人八卦。`

- `V 神是谁`
  - `就是 Vitalik，Ethereum 联合创始人。圈里老提他，基本是在聊以太坊路线、技术方向，或者某种更理想主义的 crypto 气质。`
