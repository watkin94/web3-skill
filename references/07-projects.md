# Projects

Use this file when the user asks what a chain, protocol, app, or ecosystem project is.

Do not explain projects like a whitepaper summary.
Prefer this answer shape:

1. what lane it belongs to
2. why people care
3. what people complain about
4. one simple comparison if useful

## Chains

### Bitcoin

- the original benchmark crypto asset
- usually framed more as digital hard money than as an app ecosystem
- people care because it anchors the whole market's attention and legitimacy

### Ethereum

- the main smart contract base layer in crypto history
- people care because most DeFi, NFT, L2, and on-chain culture traces back to it
- common complaint: costs and scaling pressure on the main chain

### Solana

- a fast and relatively cheap smart contract chain
- people care because the user activity, trading, meme culture, and app velocity are strong
- common complaint: stability and decentralization tradeoff debates

### BNB Chain

- exchange-adjacent smart contract ecosystem
- people care because onboarding is easy and retail traffic is large
- common complaint: strong centralization perception

### Base

- an Ethereum L2 backed by Coinbase
- people care because it is easy to onboard and has strong distribution
- common complaint: people ask whether the activity is durable or mostly incentive-driven

### Arbitrum

- major Ethereum L2
- people care because it is a large part of the L2 and DeFi conversation
- common complaint: users still get confused by bridges, governance, and fragmentation

## Protocols And Apps

### Uniswap

- the default DEX reference point for many users
- people care because it normalized on-chain swapping and AMM design
- common complaint: fees and liquidity vary by pool and chain

### Aave

- major DeFi lending protocol
- people care because it is one of the most recognizable lending names on-chain
- common complaint: the product is simple in concept, but risk is not simple in practice

### Hyperliquid

- a fast on-chain trading venue that gets mentioned a lot in trader circles
- people care because the execution experience feels closer to what traders want
- common complaint: users debate how to think about decentralization and concentration risk

### Pump.fun

- a meme coin launch platform that exploded in attention
- people care because it lowered the friction to launch and speculate on meme coins
- common complaint: the entire flow is hyper-speculative and full of low-quality launches

## Stablecoins

### USDT

- the most widely discussed stablecoin in many trading contexts
- people care because liquidity is everywhere
- common complaint: issuer and transparency questions are always part of the conversation

### USDC

- a stablecoin often seen as cleaner or more institution-friendly in image
- people care because it is widely integrated across major apps and chains
- common complaint: still has issuer and freeze risk because it is not magic internet cash

## Good Answer Pattern

- `Solana 是什么`
  - `你先把 Solana 理解成一条主打快和便宜的公链。圈里老提它，主要是因为交易和生态很活，但别人也会拿稳定性和去中心化争议来说事。`

- `Base 是什么`
  - `Base 你可以先当成 Coinbase 背后的以太坊二层。大家关注它，核心不是因为概念多新，而是因为它自带入口和分发。`
